    About Our School

    Our Vision & Mission

    Vision Statement

    We are committed to providing a Christ-centered education and empower pupils to acquire, demonstrate, articulate, and value knowledge and skills that will support them as life-long learners to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school: Respect, Tolerance, Inclusion & Excellence.

    Mission Statement

    Our mission is to help children develop and mature in a positive Christ-centered environment and prepare pupils to contribute and succeed in a rapidly changing society, enabling them to help the world become a better place.

    Respect

    Fostering mutual respect among students, staff, and community.

    Tolerance

    Embracing diversity and understanding in our learning environment.

    Inclusion

    A welcoming environment where every child feels valued and supported.

    Excellence

    Striving for the highest standards in all that we do.

    Our Aim For Students

    Building Future Leaders

    We are dedicated to developing well-rounded individuals equipped for success in life.

    Holistic Development

    Foster each child with intellectual, social, physical & moral development.

    Multiplication Mastery

    Practice and master multiplication facts for strong mathematical foundation.

    Public Speaking

    Improve public speaking skills for confident self-expression.

    Child-Focused Care

    Child focused total care education system tailored to individual needs.

    Academic Excellence

    Sound moral and academic excellence in all endeavors.

    Our Programs

    Education for Every Stage

    From early childhood through junior secondary, we provide age-appropriate learning experiences that nurture growth and prepare students for success.

    Crèche

    3 months - 2 years

    A safe and loving environment where infants receive tender care while beginning their journey of discovery through sensory play and early stimulation activities.

    • Qualified caregivers
    • Nutritious meals
    • Safe play areas
    • Parent updates

    Nursery

    2 - 5 years

    Our nursery program builds foundation skills through play-based learning, preparing children for formal education while fostering creativity and social skills.

    • Play-based learning
    • Phonics & numeracy
    • Creative arts
    • Social development

    Primary School

    6 - 11 years

    Comprehensive primary education following the Nigerian curriculum, enhanced with modern teaching methods and technology integration for 21st-century learning.

    • Core subjects
    • Computer studies
    • Music & arts
    • Physical education

    Junior Secondary

    12 - 14 years

    Preparing students for their BECE examinations with rigorous academic training, character development, and extracurricular opportunities.

    • BECE preparation
    • Science labs
    • Vocational skills
    • Sports programs
